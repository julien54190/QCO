About us

Experience, expertise and international support

Founded in 2014, Qco Group builds on more than 30 years of experience. We provide accounting, tax, legal and social secretariat services for SMEs and individuals.

Our human-sized team delivers a fast, clear and personalized follow-up tailored to your company's needs.

Also for international businesses

As an active member of Wingglobally, Qco Group relies on a global network of accountants, auditors and tax specialists.

We assist foreign companies with administrative and legal procedures in Belgium, from payroll to VAT.

Services

Our services

We support businesses and individuals with clear, efficient solutions tailored to their needs.

Accounting services

Want to delegate your company's accounting? Qco handles the core accounting tasks:

  • ›
    Bookkeeping:
    • Record your documents according to the chart of accounts
    • Prepare monthly or quarterly reporting
    • Preparation of annual accounts
  • ›Prepare an interim balance sheet
  • ›Periodic and annual VAT returns
  • ›Income tax returns
  • ›Intra-Community declarations
  • ›Intrastat declaration for STATEC
  • ›File the annual accounts with the Trade and Companies Register (LBR / eCDF)

For public limited companies, we also provide statutory auditor services.

Tax advisory

We help our clients understand and optimize their tax situation.

  • ›Analysis of the tax situation of companies and individuals
  • ›Real-estate investment
  • ›Due diligence (preliminary review)
  • ›
    Advice for individuals:
    • Purchase or sale of property
    • Creation of a civil real-estate company (SCI)
    • Change in family situation
    • Tax returns

Company formation

Have a project but need support? We assist you through every step.

  • ›Business plan creation
  • ›Liaison with a notary for drafting the articles of association
  • ›Application for a business permit
  • ›VAT registration
  • ›Registration and update of the Beneficial Owners Register (RBE)
  • ›Updates and publications with the Trade and Companies Register (LBR)

Payroll and human resources

  • ›Liaison with social security (CCSS, CNS), tax and employment authorities (ADEM)
