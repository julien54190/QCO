What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small file stored on your device when you visit a website. It helps the site function properly and improve user experience.

Types of cookies we use

  • Strictly necessary cookies: required for the website to work.
  • Analytics cookies: only if you accept them via the consent banner (not active yet).

Managing your consent
